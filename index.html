Welcome!

Hello, coders! Thank you for showing interest in the Orange County Coding Competition!

In this competition, individuals from various coding clubs from different schools around Orange County can compete in solving coding problems to earn prizes [and bragging rights]!

There will be different levels of difficulty for participants of all skill levels, ranging from those who have just gotten started in computer science to those who have been doing it for years.

The competition will be held this coming March via Zoom (or some other online medium). Let us know if this sounds interesting to you and your computer science club, and we hope to see you all there!
